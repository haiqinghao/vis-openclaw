# Asset Credits / 素材来源说明

## English

VIS OpenClaw V1.1 uses selected assets from the free portion of [Tiny Swords by Pixel Frog](https://pixelfrog-assets.itch.io/tiny-swords) for the game interface, map editor, unit animations, buildings, terrain, environment decorations, and UI-style game elements.

Current project usage is limited to the Tiny Swords Free Pack. The paid Enemy Pack is not intentionally included in this repository.

According to the Tiny Swords itch.io page, the asset pack may be used in personal and commercial projects and may be modified as needed. Credit is not required, but is welcome. The assets may not be redistributed, resold, or repackaged as standalone assets, even after modification.

Project policy:

- Keep Tiny Swords credit visible in public documentation.
- Do not sell, repackage, or distribute the Tiny Swords files as a standalone asset pack.
- Do not add paid-pack assets unless the project owner explicitly confirms the license and purchase.
- When adding third-party assets, update this file and the relevant user/developer documentation.

## 中文

VIS OpenClaw V1.1 的游戏界面、地图编辑器、单位动画、建筑、地形、环境装饰和游戏化 UI 元素，使用了 [Pixel Frog 的 Tiny Swords](https://pixelfrog-assets.itch.io/tiny-swords) 免费部分素材。

当前项目仅使用 Tiny Swords Free Pack。付费的 Enemy Pack 没有被有意包含在本仓库中。

根据 Tiny Swords 的 itch.io 页面说明，该素材包可用于个人和商业项目，并允许按项目需要进行修改；署名不是强制要求，但作者欢迎署名。素材本身不得作为独立素材被重新分发、转售或重新打包，即使已经修改也不可以。

项目约定：

- 在公开文档中保留 Tiny Swords 的素材来源说明。
- 不将 Tiny Swords 素材文件作为独立素材包出售、重新打包或分发。
- 除非项目所有者明确确认购买与授权，否则不要加入付费包素材。
- 后续新增第三方素材时，需要同步更新本文件以及相关用户/开发文档。
